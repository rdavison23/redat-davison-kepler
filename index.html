About

As an apparel designer, I have a sharp eye for color and textiles, plus solid experience in garment construction, pattern making, and styling. Blending fashion and technology opens up exciting new possibilities whether through interactive web experiences, e-commerce, or digital fashion projects. I am always eager to learn, push boundaries, and take on challenges with fresh ideas and determination.
